Soul: Handcrafted Skateboards and Cruisers

Soul is a young skateboard merchandise brand established in 2011 in Ukraine, Lviv.

The founder and an active member of the skate community-Yura Futalo took notice that back in the day there was a lack for minimalistic clothing for skaters, there were not much of interesting cuts or designs, the materials were not always great as well.

It added up to a serious passion for skateboarding and everything started with making t-shirts with a logo.

Therefore, the logo and the brand name idea Soul came up very quickly. It symbolizes freedom, friendship, unity and all good sorts of things.

Soul is for those who appreciate the high-quality skateboard merch and value the unique experience of skateboarding!

From Soul to soul-is one of our mottos. Skateboarding changed my life and many other lives, I'm sure. After t-shirts, we started to make beanies, after beanies-finally skateboards and cruises. We work with every shape, color and form. We can make longboards too, or even add Swarovski crystals if the client wishes so and are open for creative and fun cooperation!

Also, Soul organizes events in Lviv, namely Go Skate Day, Business Chill for those who like to skate or to join the skate movement and philosophy.

Go ride your skateboard and change the world!
